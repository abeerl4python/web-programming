CHARACTERS

Eleven

A teen girl with telepathic and psychokinetic abilities, a result of being one of Dr. Brenner's subjects from Hawkins National Laboratory.

Mike

Middle child of Karen and Ted Wheeler, brother of Nancy and Holly, and one of three friends of Will Byers. He is an intelligent and conscientious student and is committed to his friends.

Dustin Hunderson

One of Will Byers' friends. His cleidocranial dysplasia causes him to lisp.

Will Byers

The son of Joyce Byers and younger brother of Jonathan Byers. He is captured by a monster from the "Upside Down", an alternate dimension discovered by Hawkins Laboratory scientists.

Max Mayfeild

A tomboy who catches the attention of both Lucas and Dustin, and is targeted by Vecna.

Nancy Wheeler

Studious and rule-abiding, Nancy finds another side of herself while investigating the Hawkins Lab and the death of her friend Barbara.
