Classic Fudgy Brownie Recipe

These brownie are quintessential treat, perfect for chocolate lovers. They are dense and fudgy with deep chocolate, complimented with slight chewiness around the corners

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup (35g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (64g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chopped nuts or chocolate chips

Method:

  1. Preheat and Prep:Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Melt Butter and Sugar:Melt the butter and mix with sugar until combined.
  3. Add Wet ingredients:Add egges and vanilla essence to the mixture and beat well.
  4. Mix Dry ingredients:In a seperate bowl, sift together cocoa powder, flour, salt and baking powder.
  5. Combine Wet and Dry ingredients:Fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and add chopped nuts or chocolate chips if using.
  6. Bake:Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Cool and Cut:Let the brownies cool completely before cutting into squares.

    8. Tip:Don't overbake to ensure fudgy brownies and let them cool completely before serving for better texture.
